About Us

Welcome to How Many of Me — your go-to platform for discovering how many people in the United States share your name. Whether you're exploring your identity, satisfying curiosity, or just having fun, our name analysis tool offers a unique look into population data and personal identity.

Our Mission

We aim to make data meaningful, accessible, and enjoyable. By transforming publicly available statistics into easy-to-understand insights, we help people connect with their names and communities in a new and exciting way.

What We Offer

Using reliable data from sources like the U.S. Census Bureau, our tool estimates how many individuals in the U.S. share both your first and last name. With a few clicks, you can uncover name popularity, uniqueness, and surprising facts — all from a simple search.

Why It Matters

Names are more than just labels — they carry history, culture, and personal stories. Understanding how common or rare your name is can build a sense of identity, connection, and even pride. Our tool brings this experience to your fingertips in an engaging, accurate, and informative way.

Our Commitment to Trust and Accuracy

Our team is composed of data professionals, developers, and user experience designers who are committed to delivering a trustworthy, accurate, and user-friendly platform. We continuously refine our methods to ensure the information we provide is both reliable and easy to understand.

Experience You Can Trust

With a deep understanding of data analytics and public information systems, we strive to offer a platform that meets the highest standards of integrity. We follow best practices for transparency, data sourcing, and user privacy to meet expectations from users and search engines alike.

Get Involved
